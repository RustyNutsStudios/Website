You’re an Egg. They’re Eggs. Everyone’s Eggs.

And they’re all coming to crack you open.

Break into chaos as you fend off waves of egg-saulting enemies, dodge frying pans, and level up like you’ve just downed a pint at the local. Customize your Egg with wild traits, wield egg-splosive weapons, and master an elemental system that’s hotter than a full English breakfast.

Dodge, duck, and dive through 20 rounds of egg-hausting action. Every move, explosion, & gun shot is synced to the beat—because even unhidnged destuction needs a soundtrack. Built in Unreal Engine (you know, the most powerful 3D engine that makes games hyper-realistic games look), this game uses all that tech to bring you the most cinematic 2D pixalted eggs of mass destruction, that can now mother clucking JUMP! We’ve cracked open a whole new dimension of choas to the genre. Turning up the heat on your dopamine receptors until they’re perfectly fried.
