About

Rusty Nuts Studios

Solo dev studio based in Tbilisi, Georgia. Just trying to make fun games in Unreal Engine 5. Two projects in the works right now — one where you control the weather to fight hordes of enemies, and one where passive-aggressive eggs kill each other over nothing.

Both built in UE5 with PaperZD, mixing 2D pixel art with 3D lighting because it looks cool and nobody else is really doing it.

Hard Boiled Climate Change

In Development

Control the Weather.
Dominate the Horde.

Top-Down Horde Arena Shooter • 2.5D • UE5 + PaperZD

A horde arena shooter where you control the weather to dominate the battlefield. Set rain, wind, temperature, and visibility before each wave — the battlefield transforms, the horde adapts, and every weapon synergy shifts. Over 30 interconnected weather controls, 50 unlockable characters, and a cascade system where changing one condition changes everything.

Also in Development

Nidgets

Find Your Inner Peace
With Mindless Violence.

Idle Chaos Simulator • Low-Poly • UE5

Drop into a colorful low-poly world as a Nidget — a small, passive-aggressive creature with no purpose and even less patience. Weapons fall from the sky. Other Nidgets spawn in. Chaos builds slowly, then all at once. Grab whatever drops. Kill a Nidget, they drop everything. Die anyway. Respawn. Float back as a ghost. Watch the mayhem from above.

Weather shifts on its own — or nudge it yourself. Rain to snow to desert to ice. No goals. No skill required. Just lofi beats, shifting weather, and the zen-like satisfaction of passive-aggressive carnage.

Idle / Active Hybrid Dynamic Weather Lofi Soundtrack Ghost Spectating Zero Skill Required
